Formula 1: The Pinnacle of Motorsport

Experience the Adrenaline of F1 Racing

Formula 1 is the ultimate speed experience. With cars that can reach over 370 km/h and navigate tight corners with precision, each race is a heart-pounding display of skill and bravery.

Innovation at the Heart of Formula 1

F1 is where cutting-edge technology meets the racetrack. Every car is a marvel of engineering, featuring advanced aerodynamics, hybrid power units, and state-of-the-art materials.

The World's Premier Racing Series

Formula 1 is more than just a race; it's a global phenomenon. With Grands Prix in over 20 countries, F1 showcases a blend of culture, innovation, and competition.

Inside an F1 Race Weekend

Immerse yourself in the excitement of an F1 race weekend. Starting with practice sessions on Friday, followed by the critical qualifying on Saturday, and culminating in the high-stakes race on Sunday, each day offers a unique blend of strategy, skill, and drama. Witness how teams and drivers prepare and strategize for the ultimate showdown on race day.

F1's Commitment to Sustainability

Formula 1 is leading the way in automotive sustainability. With a goal to achieve net-zero carbon emissions by 2030, the sport is embracing green technologies, including hybrid power units and sustainable fuel. Explore how F1 is balancing its thrilling racing with a commitment to environmental responsibility, shaping a greener future for motorsport.

The Icons of Formula 1

Formula 1 has been home to some of the greatest drivers in history. Legends like Ayrton Senna, Michael Schumacher, and Lewis Hamilton have left an indelible mark on the sport with their incredible talent and fierce competitiveness. Discover their remarkable careers and the milestones that defined their legacy in the world of F1 racing.
